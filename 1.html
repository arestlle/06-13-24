A Letter From Denden

Dear PAPA, Let me start my greeting with an expression of gratitude. I want to thank you for being a good father to us, a father who is always there to support, guide, and correct us. I want to thank you for providing for all our needs, no matter how small or big they are. The same goes for providing me and Coco with supervision and care whenever we are sick. Those were the times that we needed you the most, and you were always there, so thank you. I also want to express my gratitude for the continuous support for my studies and everyday needs in school. I know these are the words that we rarely say to you, but we mean them every time we do. We are not very vocal with these, so we hope that through this letter we are able to express them to you. Also, I would like to say sorry for everything we have done that has upset and disappointed you. In return, we would like to make up for them in other ways, such as by dedicating our achievements in school to you. Everything I and Coco have achieved, we all dedicate to you. We acknowledge your sacrifice for us, papa, and for that, we thank you. Happy happy birthday, papa. We wish you good health and many more birthdays to come. We love you.
